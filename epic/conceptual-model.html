Conceptual Model

Entities & Attributes:

PROFILE:

  • profileId (primary key)
  • profileActivationToken
  • profileEmail
  • profileHash
  • profileHandle

CONTENT:

  • contentId (primary key)
  • contentName
  • contentImg
  • contentFactoid

LIKE:

  • likeProfileId (foreign key)
  • likeContentId (foreign key)

Relations:

  • One profile can like many cards (content) - (1 to n)
  • Many cards (content) can be liked by many profiles (m to n)
