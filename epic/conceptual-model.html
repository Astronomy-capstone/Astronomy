Conceptual Model

Entities & Attributes:

GALLERY (strong)

  • galleryId (primary key)
  • galleryName
  • galleryDate

GLOSSARY (strong):

  • glossaryId (primary key)
  • glossaryName
  • contentImg
  • contentFactoid

VIDEO (weak):

  • videoId (primary key)
  • videoImgId (foreign key)
  • videoDateId

NEWS (weak):

  • newsId (primary key)
  • newsImgId (foreign key)
  • newsDate
  • newsEvent

Relations:

  • Many gallery items can have many news items
  • Many gallery items can have many glossary entries
  • One video can have many glossary entries
