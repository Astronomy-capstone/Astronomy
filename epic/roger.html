Mr. Trujillo

Name: Roger Trujillo

Age: 47

Gender: Male

Occupation: Middle school teacher

Technology: iPhone 8, Asus Chromebook 14 (Chrome OS) (work machine), Mac Pro desktop


Attitude:

  • Likes to keep track of astronomical events, watches a little UFO stuff on YouTube and talk radio but isn’t that weird about it
  • Is on social media with a small number of enthusiastic friends with similar interests; likes to share anything about celestial events
  • Overworked but has a lot of enthusiasm about teaching

Needs/Goals/Features:

  • Wants an accessible tool to share with his students and encourage interest in astronomy
  • Needs simple interface that doesn’t require too much tech experience

Frustrations:

  • Lack of decent/accessible app to educate students with factoids and additional info
  • Simple interface for students

Goals:

  • He wants to be able to show information to his students in classroom and web conferencing settings.

User Story:

As a user, I need an easy resource that students can take advantage of on their own with little instruction needed outside the classroom.


Use Case:

Title:Show his students how to subscribe to our website for astronomy news

Description:He wants to go to the site and demonstrate entering his email address to sign up for news updates

Name Of User & Role:Roger Trujillo, teacher

Usage Preconditions:Must navigate to website and have a valid email address

Usage Postconditions:Roger receives periodic news updates through his email


Use Interaction Flow:

  • Roger enters URL into web browser
  • Server returns website main page
  • Roger clicks Subscribe in the navbar
  • Server displays a small drop-down form asking for his email address
  • Roger types in his email and clicks Subscribe
  • Server receives email address and stores it
  • Server displays alert that his subscription was successful
  • Roger receives periodic news updates in his email inbox
